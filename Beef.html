Beef pulao recipe

  1. To make Beef Pulao, first cut 1 kg beef into small pieces. Wash and soak 2 cups of basmati rice for 30 minutes. Heat oil in a pan and sauté 2 medium onions until golden brown.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger and garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute. Add the beef pieces and cook until browned.
  3. Add 2 medium tomatoes, 1 teaspoon cumin powder, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour or until the beef is tender.
  5. In a separate pot, heat ghee and add the soaked rice. Sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add the cooked beef mixture and mix well. Add garam masala powder and mix. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  6. Serve hot.
