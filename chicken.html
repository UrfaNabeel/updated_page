chicken biryani recipe

  1. Wash and soak 2 cups of basmati rice for 30 minutes. Heat oil in a pan and sauté 2 medium onions until golden brown. Add ginger and garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute. Add 1 kg chicken pieces and cook until browned
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and add 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked
  3. In a separate pot, heat ghee and add the soaked rice. Sauté for 1-2 minutes, then add the cooked chicken mixture and mix well. Add garam masala powder, saffron, and mix. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander leaves and mint leaves
